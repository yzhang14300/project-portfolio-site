Use ↑↓←→ of your keyboard to contorl your character. You have three initial hp, a heart will spawn on the screen every 10s. Heart will increase your hp by 1, touch a bug will decrease your hp by 3. If you hp reach zero, game will reset. When you reach the river, your score will increase 1, two scores will spawn a random gem . When you gain all three colors of gems, a star will spawn. Each star grant you the power to kill a bug. When all the bugs are killed, a key will spawn on the scree. Take the key to win the game. Have fun!
